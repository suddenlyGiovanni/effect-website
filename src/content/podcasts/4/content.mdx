In this episode of Cause & Effect, Johannes Schickling is joined by Dillon Mulroy, Domains Lead at [Vercel](https://vercel.com), who shares his personal journey with Effect and how Vercel gradually adopted it across their Domains platform. Dillon explains why Effect feels like having superpowers for error handling, observability, testing, and more.
