## Episode Notes

In this episode, Attila Večerek, Tech Lead & Staff Engineer at Zendesk,
joins our host Johannes Schickling to discuss how Zendesk incrementally adopted
Effect in a polyglot environment with a large codebase.

Effect is an ecosystem of tools to build production-grade software in TypeScript.
